FAQs

How do I upload files? To upload files, make sure they are in one of the following formats: PDF, JPG, JPEG, or PNG. Then, select the "Choose File" option to upload your files.

How can I save my files? Narrify does not save any files. They are kept temporarily to avoid storing information that may be subject to copyright issues.

What should I do if my text does not extract? Ensure that your file is in the correct format, has clear text, and that the image is not blurry.

Features

Narrify extracts text from PDF, JPG, JPEG, and PNG files using pdf.js for PDFs and Tesseract.js for OCR. It converts text to speech with Microsoft voice support, allowing custom voice selection, play, pause, resume, and cancel functions while tracking reading position. A progress indicator, alerts, dynamic button controls, and error handling enhance the user experience.
